Mondo Subscription Tracker

Connect your Mondo account to see recurring subscriptions and bills.

Optimise your monthly outgoings, get notified if you're not going to have enough money to pay a subscription, and get notified if a cancelled subscription tries to bill you again.

Built with Thomas Grainger and John Sandall at the 3rd Mondo hackathon.

Mondo Loyalty Balances

Connect your Mondo account to your Nectar or Marks & Spencer loyalty scheme accounts to see your balances in your account feed.

Whenever you make a purchase at Sainsburys or M&S, your new balance will be put into your Mondo feed, and you'll receive a push notification.

Build at the 2nd Mondo hackathon.

jquery.complexify.js

Complexify makes it easy to inform users about the security of the passwords they are choosing on your site.

Instead of requiring numbers, capital letters, or punctuation, Complexify measures password quality based on the computational difficulty to break it.

django-google-charts

django-google-charts provides a pure Python API for creating charts for rendering in Django templates.

As charts are normal Python objects they can be subclassed and inherited just like any other, allowing for code re-use, while also abstracting away the details of rendering in order to provide a secure and easy way to render charts.
